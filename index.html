Ferrari LaFerrari

Hybrid Power

LaFerrari uses a V12 engine combined with electric motors for extreme performance and better efficiency.

Top Speed

This hypercar reaches speeds over 217 mph, putting it in the league of the world’s fastest cars.

Exclusivity

Only 499 coupe versions were made, making the LaFerrari a rare and highly collectible masterpiece.

Design

The sleek, aerodynamic body improves speed and handling while giving LaFerrari its signature aggressive look.

Technology

Advanced F1-inspired systems control traction, suspension, and braking to maximize performance on and off track.

Made in Italy

LaFerrari was designed and built in Maranello, Italy—home of Ferrari’s most iconic automotive innovations.
